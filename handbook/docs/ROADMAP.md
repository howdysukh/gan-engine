# Long-Term Vision

Future versions aim to expand Gan Engine into a comprehensive market monitoring platform focused on transparent mathematical analysis, user-controlled alerts, and a distraction-free investing experience.

The project will continue to prioritize explainable calculations over prediction-based systems, ensuring that every insight remains understandable, reproducible, and grounded in observable market data.
