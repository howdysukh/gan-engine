# Frequently Asked Questions

## Does Gan Engine predict stock prices?

No.

Gan Engine performs mathematical monitoring based on the Gann Square of Nine methodology. It does not attempt to forecast future market prices.

---

## Which markets are supported?

Currently, the application focuses on Indian equities listed on the NSE and BSE through Yahoo Finance symbols.

---

## Can I use Gan Engine without a Google account?

Yes.

Guest sessions allow users to explore the application without linking a Google account.

---

## Does Gan Engine provide financial advice?

No.

The project is intended for educational and analytical purposes only.

---

## Is my portfolio stored?

Portfolio management is planned for a future release.
