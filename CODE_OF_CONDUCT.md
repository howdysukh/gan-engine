# Code of Conduct

Gan Engine welcomes contributions from everyone.

We are committed to maintaining a respectful, collaborative, and inclusive environment for all contributors.

## Our Standards

- Be respectful.
- Be constructive.
- Assume good intentions.
- Welcome feedback.
- Focus on improving the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment
- Personal attacks
- Discrimination
- Spam
- Malicious contributions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove contributions or restrict participation if these guidelines are violated.
