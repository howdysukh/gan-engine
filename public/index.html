ABOUT GAN ENGINE
Gan Engine continuously monitors live NSE market prices using the mathematical principles of the Gann Square of Nine.

It does not predict future prices. Instead, it calculates significant mathematical support and resistance levels, then monitors the market in real time whenever those levels are reached.
